# Spain-First Launch Plan

## Current build

The static MVP now includes:

- a landing page
- 5 separate SEO-focused generator pages
- copy-to-clipboard flow
- print-to-PDF flow
- disclaimers and FAQ blocks per page
- deployment basics: `robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, `404.html`, `README.md`, `netlify.toml`

## Why Spain

- bigger search opportunity than Portugal
- competition still looks more open than France or Germany
- clear transactional and informational search intent
